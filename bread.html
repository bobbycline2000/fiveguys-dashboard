How the math works. Every $986 in sales = 1 hamburger tray. Every $19,974 = 1 hot dog tray. Build-to = total covered sales ÷ tray rate, rounded up. Order = build-to − on-hand. Hot dogs round small because of their 5-day shelf life.

Lock rules (KY-2065). Mon delivery locks Thu 2 PM. Wed locks Mon 2 PM. Fri locks Wed 2 PM. Sat locks Thu 2 PM. After lock, the order is final.

How to use it. Pre-place all four orders on Sunday in BOSS (your best guess). Each day before the lock, open this page, count on-hand, confirm/edit projections, and click Adjust Order in BOSS to match the suggestion.
