How the math works

Tray rates: every $986 in net sales = 1 HB tray (5 bags). Every $19,974 in net sales = 1 HD tray (6 bags).

Per delivery: we sum the CT-projected sales for the days that delivery covers (Mon covers Mon+Tue, Wed covers Wed+Thu, Fri covers Fri, Sat covers Sat+Sun), add a $3K sales buffer (your safety cushion — leaves ~15 HB bags on the shelf at the end of every window), figure how many bags you need, subtract what's already on the shelf, round up to whole trays.

Forward chaining: the tool simulates each delivery in order — what you have now + what arrives Mon − what gets used Mon+Tue = what's left for Wed, and so on. So a single count drives the whole week's orders.
