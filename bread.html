How the math works. Every $986 in sales = 1 hamburger tray. Every $19,974 = 1 hot dog tray. Those numbers come from your store's last 8 weeks of POS data — not a guess. Build-to = sales window ÷ tray rate, rounded up. Order = build-to − on-hand. Hot dogs round small because of the 5-day shelf life.
